Electronic descriptors calculation (last revision - 14 June 2007)

For electronic descriptors WinMOPAC is launched and calculates following descriptors

  • FINAL HEAT OF FORMATION
  • IONIZATION POTENTIAL,
  • ELECTRONIC ENERGY,
  • MOLECULAR WEIGHT,
  • eHOMO,
  • CORE-CORE REPULSION,
  • eLUMO,
  • TOTAL ENERGY
