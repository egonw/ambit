In the first step of your ToxPredict workflow you are invited to select the structure(s) for which you would like to apply some OpenTox model(s). The Draw and Upload options below provide different ways for structure selection:
  • Draw: You can draw a chemical structure in the built-in editor and upload into your workspace.
  • Upload: You can upload your own dataset(s); ToxPredict accepts SDF, MOL, CSV and RDF input file formats, among many others.
Once you have entered your query string, chemical structure drawing or file name for upload, press Upload to proceed to the following step of your ToxPredict workflow.
